Hurricanes

If you want to learn the inner working of hurricanes just make a cup of coffee!! Yep you read that right. When you make that fresh cup stir it vigorously and watch the magic

 

You see the atmosphere above you head may be air but it is fluid and acts much like a fluid. Just like coffee, or any other liquid it is a representative microcosm of other natural phenomena. You can pick out some obvious feature is the scup of joe that you would see in a real hurricane.

Feeder bands, the CDO (central dense overcast) that is in the middle where the eye would be.
